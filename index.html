Word-by-word changes between Federal Reserve policy statements
How to use this tracker

Each position on the slider represents one FOMC meeting. Drag left or right to move between statements. The selected statement is shown with changes highlighted compared to the previous meeting: green words were added and red strikethrough words were removed. The voting record appears below the policy text in lighter type with the same highlighting. The earliest statement in the series is the baseline and shows no comparison.
